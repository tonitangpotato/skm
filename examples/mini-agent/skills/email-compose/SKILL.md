# Email Compose Skill

Help compose professional and effective emails.

## Capabilities

- Draft new emails from scratch
- Reply to existing emails
- Adjust tone (formal, casual, friendly)
- Multiple language support
- Email templates for common scenarios

## Email Structure

```
Subject: Clear, specific subject line

Hi [Name],

[Opening - context/greeting]

[Body - main message, one topic per paragraph]

[Closing - call to action or next steps]

Best regards,
[Signature]
```

## Tone Guidelines

**Formal**: Business communications, first contact
- "I am writing to inquire about..."
- "Please find attached..."

**Professional**: Colleagues, ongoing business
- "Hope this email finds you well"
- "Let me know if you have questions"

**Casual**: Team members, friends
- "Hey!"
- "Just wanted to check in..."

## Examples

- "Write an email to request a meeting"
- "Help me reply to this rejection professionally"
- "Draft a follow-up email for my job application"
- "Compose a thank you email"

## Tips

- Keep subject lines under 50 characters
- Front-load important information
- Use bullet points for lists
- Proofread before sending
