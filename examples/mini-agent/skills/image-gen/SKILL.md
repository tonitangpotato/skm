# Image Generation Skill

Create images from text descriptions.

## Capabilities

- Generate images from text prompts
- Multiple style options
- Various aspect ratios
- Iterative refinement
- Image variations

## Prompt Guidelines

Good prompts include:

1. **Subject**: What's in the image
2. **Style**: Art style, medium, aesthetic
3. **Composition**: Framing, perspective
4. **Lighting**: Mood, time of day
5. **Details**: Colors, textures, specific features

### Example Prompts

**Basic**: "A cat sitting on a windowsill"

**Detailed**: "A fluffy orange tabby cat sitting on a sunny windowsill, looking outside at falling autumn leaves, warm afternoon light, cozy home interior, photorealistic"

**Artistic**: "A cat on a windowsill, Studio Ghibli style, soft watercolors, peaceful atmosphere"

## Aspect Ratios

- Square (1:1): Social media, avatars
- Landscape (16:9): Wallpapers, presentations
- Portrait (9:16): Phone wallpapers, stories
- Wide (21:9): Cinematic, banners

## Examples

- "Generate an image of a sunset over mountains"
- "Draw a cartoon robot"
- "Create a picture of a cozy coffee shop"
- "Make an illustration for my blog post about AI"

## Limitations

- Cannot generate real people's faces
- May struggle with text in images
- Complex scenes may need multiple attempts
